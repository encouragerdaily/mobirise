We're All About Encouragement

In the Bible, the word encourage is translated from a word that means to strengthen. That said, Encourager Daily is a social media devotional that exists to strengthen brothers and sisters in Christ who may be struggling. Whether you are going through confusing times, exhausting situations, or seemingly hopeless seasons, we're here to share the love of Jesus Christ by way of the Word of God.

We value His love, His presence, and His truth and seek to express each facet of God through our devotions. We know firsthand what it's like to go through situations and feel confused and alone. Yet, we are never alone because God promised never to leave us nor forsake us. With that in mind, we intend to remind you and stir you up as you walk towards your destiny!
